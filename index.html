Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cleaning services.

You can schedule a cleaning service by contacting us through our website, phone, or email. We'll work with you to find a convenient time that fits your schedule. For regular cleaning plans, we'll establish a consistent schedule that works for you.

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. We understand that emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes to your appointment.

Our cleaners bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you prefer that we use specific products due to allergies or preferences, please let us know in advance. For monthly plans, basic supplies are included, while annual plans include all cleaning supplies.

Yes, we offer eco-friendly cleaning options. Our green cleaning products are effective, safe for your family and pets, and better for the environment. If you prefer eco-friendly cleaning, please select this option when booking your service.

All our cleaning staff undergo thorough background checks and reference verification before joining our team. They are also trained in our cleaning procedures and customer service standards. We prioritize hiring reliable, trustworthy, and skilled professionals to ensure the highest quality service.

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, please contact us within 24 hours of your cleaning, and we'll return to re-clean any areas of concern at no additional cost. We value your feedback as it helps us improve our services.
